Gestalt

Design a logo using at least on of Gestalt's visual principles

The principles that I chose were closure and proximity.

Closure

Closure is a common design technique that uses the human eye's tendency to see closed shapes. Closure works where an object is incomplete or the interior space of an element is not fully closed, but the viewer perceives a complete shape by filling in the missing information.

Proximity

Proximity uses the close arrangement of elements to create a group association between those objects. If individual elements are also similar, they will tend to be perceived as a single whole, even though they are separate elements.

I think I successfully implemented both closure and proximity in my logo. Although the shapes used are not the same, they are similar in terms of being curved. My use of proximity is successful and everyone that I showed the design to could recognise that it was a cowboy. Although the logo itself does not convey the fact that it is whiskey, I do not believe that this is necessary because most logos do not convey what products they brand (i.e. Addidas, WWF, IBM)
