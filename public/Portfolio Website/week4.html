I attempted to record sounds that sounded similar to a drum kit, this way I could make a song without the use of premade music loops. I have always wanted to create something similar to this, however my skill of Logic Pro/Avelon Pro/FL Studio etc. is not significant enough to create something as complex. Therefore I opted to aim for something ambitious but achievable within the time frame (and with only 10 sounds).
