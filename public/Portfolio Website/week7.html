Style

The slides are all created in illustrator, however, I wanted to retain the hand drawn style as a nod to how storyboards had always been made before the digital age.

Storyboards are meant to be simple but easy to see what each panel represents. Therefore, I limited myself to a greyscale palette and red arrows to indicate direction of movement.
