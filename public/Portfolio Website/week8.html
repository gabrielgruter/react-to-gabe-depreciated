Organic Compositions

Use only triangles and squares to create a organic/informal composition. You can have different types of triangles, but the shapes cannot be changed other than colour, size, and rotation. (no rounded edges etc.)

Idea

I wanted to use gradients to create a tunnel effect and use rotation to create the illusion of curves, when in reality there are only squares and triangles being used.

The gradient changes from deep red to orange, although the difference is slight, it creates harmony and the illusion that the path in the tunnel is glowing as if there is a light source. Each square has an opacity of 50% and is slightly larger than the previous one. The layers cause the opacity to change the closer you get to the centre, thus giving the illusion of a tunnel. Each sqaure is also rotated by a small degree to create the illusion of a spiral and create the triangle shapes on the walls of the tunnel. The negative space around the tunnel also forms the shape of four triangles because the image at its core is a black square overlayed by a red square rotated 90 degrees.
